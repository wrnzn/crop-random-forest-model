Input Units Guide

  • N, P, K: Enter nutrient concentration in mg/kg (ppm). Keep all three in the same unit.
  • Temperature: Celsius (°C).
  • Humidity: Percentage (%).
  • pH: 0–14 scale.
  • Rainfall: Millimeters (mm) annual total.

Note: If your soil report uses different units (e.g., kg/ha), convert to mg/kg for consistency. Mixing units may distort predictions.
